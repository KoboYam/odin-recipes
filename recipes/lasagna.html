Lasagna

Description

This tasty Italian staple can me made my anyone. As long as you have 45 mins to spare and ample motivation you can make this yourself at home. This recipe is so basic you might not even need to run to the store and it's taste will leave even the most stringent of Italian Grandmas impressed with it's bold flavor. If this sounds good to you the lets get started.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 gloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • a pinch of basil
  • lasagna noodles
  • 3/4 pound of mozzarella
  • 3/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage on a skillet and season to taste
  2. Then add in the crushed tomatoes and paste into the meat
  3. add the garlic, basil and sugar into the skillet and stir in
  4. let mixture simmer on stove for 14 mins on medium heat
  5. boil lasagna noodles until cooked
  6. In a pan start layering the noodles,meat and cheese in alternating fashion
  7. top of with and extra sprinkle of cheese on top
  8. let lasagna cooked for 40 mins on 350f heat
  9. Take out lasagna and let in cool for 15 mins
  10. The last step is to plate it and enjoy!
