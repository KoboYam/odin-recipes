Description

Whats the perfect treat enjoy on a nice summer day or any day at that ? The answer if you didn't know is key lime pie.This tasty treat originated from the Florida Keys hence the name key lime pie. The recipe works best with authentic key lime but you'll be sure to love this recipe no matter what variant you get your hands on !

Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup key lim juice
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon grated lime zest
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degress F
  2. Combine condensed milk,key lime juice,sour cream, and lime zest in a bowl; mix well and pour into crust
  3. bake in oven for 5 to 8 mins until small bubbles form
  4. Place in fridge and chill it till firm
  5. Plate your pie and enjoy!
